Why be a developer

By Amber Oglesby

Flex that Creativity!

Enrolling in a Web development course has given me th opportunity to utilize my creativity. There is always something new to learn, and I enjoy the challeges that it poses.Creativity focuses on the quality of work and the development of new ideas and that is something i am looking forward too.

Employment Opportunities

Web development offers many career opportunities. Getting into web development can make for a flexible work schedule, and offers work from home opportunities. It is also new to me and exciting.

The way of the Future

Technological advances never cease to amaze me. Web development specifically always offers a new challenge.Jobs in tech offer a wide range of career opportunities and will likely be in high demand in the future.
